Game Description:

Your massive cruiser has been sucked into a black hole, and now you're stranded in a distant corner of the universe, near a mysterious and unknown planet. But that's not your only problem. This strange and dangerous sector of space is under attack by alien invaders! In Invasion of Space Aliens, you take control of a powerful assault ship, tasked with gathering platinum from asteroids to repair your cruiser and find a way back home, all while battling endless waves of enemies and deadly bosses.

Say goodbye to peaceful flights! Alien hordes are relentless and will stop at nothing to destroy you. You'll need sharp piloting skills, weapon upgrades, and resource collection to survive. Uncover the secrets of this strange planet while fighting for your life.

For now, the game offers an endless survival mode, where the difficulty ramps up with each wave of enemies. But that's just the beginning! Future updates will include new missions, diverse bosses, interactive cutscenes, and even more space to explore.

Key Features:

  • Black hole and mysterious planet — You’ve ended up in an unknown part of the universe, full of dangers and secrets.
  • Endless survival mode — How long can you last against the growing alien threat?
  • Epic boss battles — Each enemy is unique and requires different strategies to defeat.
  • Assault ship upgrades — Gather platinum and upgrade your ship to become even stronger.
  • Future updates — New missions, levels, cutscenes, and alien threats are on the way!

Controls:

  • WASD — Move your ship through space.
  • F — Turn on the spotlight to illuminate your surroundings.
  • TAB — Open the shop to purchase upgrades.
  • ESC — Open the settings menu.
  • Space — Skip dialogues (in future updates).
  • Shift (hold) — Manually target enemies for your drones.
  • Alt - Show drone health.

A black hole has thrown you into an unknown dimension. Can you survive the alien invasion and find your way back home? Strap in and start your cosmic adventure now!
